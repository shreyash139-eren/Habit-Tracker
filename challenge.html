100 Push-Up Challenge

Join the Movement. 100 Push-Ups a Day. One Goal. All In.

Here's What You’ll Do:

  • Complete 100 push-ups every day for 21 days straight.
  • After completing your daily push-ups, return here and mark the day as done.
  • Everyone who successfully completes the full 21-day challenge will earn a fixed reward in coins.
  • Three participants will be randomly selected to receive exciting bumper gifts!
  • All participants will receive a special participation reward — no one leaves empty-handed.
  • Join a community of fitness enthusiasts cheering each other on.
  • Get a digital badge on your profile upon completion!

Are you ready to push your limits and be part of something powerful? Let’s go!

Hydration Challenge

Drink More. Feel Better. Stay Consistent.

Here’s What You’ll Do:

  • Drink at least 4 liters of water every day for 21 days.
  • Come back daily to log your water intake and mark your progress.
  • Those who complete all 21 days will earn a fixed coin reward.
  • Three lucky winners will be randomly selected for exciting bumper prizes!
  • Everyone who joins the challenge will receive a participation reward.
  • Get hydration tips and reminders to keep you on track.
  • Stay motivated with community support and daily encouragement.
  • Earn a digital "Hydration Hero" badge on your profile upon completion.

Small habits lead to big results. Start your hydration journey today!
